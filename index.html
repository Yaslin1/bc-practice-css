Cosmic Covergence

An event where comic book enthusiasts, artists, writers, and fans from all over the world come together to celebrate the vibrant and diverse world of comics. The event features panels, workshops, cosplay competitions, artist signings, and exhibitions, providing a platform for networking, learning, and engaging in the comic book community. Whether you're a seasoned collector, an aspiring artist, or simply a fan, Comic Convergence offers an immersive experience where the magic of comics truly comes to life.
